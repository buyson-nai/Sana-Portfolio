ANALYSTS

INTJ - The Architect

Introduction

INTJs are logical, strategic, and determined to make their visions a reality. They are often ahead of their time.

Strengths

  • Curious
  • Rational
  • Independent

Weaknesses

  • Arrogant
  • Prone to Burnout
  • Emotionally Detached

INTP - The Logician

Introduction

INTPs are analytical and objective thinkers, constantly searching for truth and knowledge in the world around them.

Strengths

  • Honest
  • Open-minded
  • Thrist of Knowledge

Weaknesses

  • Distractible
  • Overthinker
  • Avoidant of Conflict

ENTJ - The Commander

Introduction

ENTJs are decisive, strategic, and highly driven individuals who are always focused on achieving their long-term goals.

Strengths

  • Confident
  • Ambitious
  • Competitive

Weaknesses

  • Impatient of Slowness
  • Tendency to Overwork
  • Controlling or Overbearing

ENTP - The Debater

Introduction

ENTPs are innovative, curious, and enjoy engaging in debates and discussions to challenge ideas and explore new possibilities.

Strengths

  • Energetic
  • Quick Thinker
  • Big-Picture Focused

Weaknesses

  • Argumentative
  • Easily Distracted
  • Dislike Practical Matters

DIPLOMATS

INFJ - The Advocate

Introduction

INFJs are insightful, visionary, and committed to helping others. They seek to make the world a better place.

Strengths

  • Passionate
  • Deep Thinker
  • Goal-Oriented

Weaknesses

  • Sensitive to Criticism
  • Can be Overly Private
  • Tend to Bottle Up Emotions

INFP - The Mediator

Introduction

INFPs are idealists who are deeply committed to their values and helping others. They seek authenticity and meaning in their lives.

Strengths

  • Generous
  • Empathetic
  • Nonjudgmental

Weaknesses

  • Self-Isolating
  • Too Eager to Please
  • Emotionally Vulnerable

ENFJ - The Protagonist

Introduction

ENFJs are natural-born leaders who inspire and motivate others. They care deeply about people and their well-being.

Strengths

  • Reliable
  • Receptive
  • Encouraging

Weaknesses

  • Unrealistic
  • Overly Idealistic
  • Tends to Overexted Themselves

ENFP - The Campaigner

Introduction

ENFPs are enthusiastic, creative, and open-minded individuals who are motivated by new possibilities and their personal values.

Strengths

  • Easygoing
  • Good-Natured and Positive
  • Adaptable and Open to New Ideas

Weaknesses

  • Restless
  • Overly Accomodating
  • Can Struggle with Structure

SENTINELS

ISTJ - The Logistician

Introduction

ISTJs are practical and fact-minded individuals, whose reliability cannot be doubted. They prefer structure and consistency in their lives.

Strengths

  • Practical
  • Disciplined
  • Detail-Oriented

Weaknesses

  • Insensitive
  • Judgmental
  • Overly Rule-Focused

ISFJ - The Defender

Introduction

ISFJs are nurturers who are deeply committed to their families and communities. They value tradition and loyalty.

Strengths

  • Loyal
  • Supporive
  • Hardworking

Weaknesses

  • Overly Humble
  • Reluctant to Change
  • Can be Overwhelmed by Criticism

ESTJ - The Executive

Introduction

ESTJs are organized, efficient, and focused on achieving goals. They value structure and are often seen as natural leaders.

Strengths

  • Dedicated
  • Strong-willed
  • Enjoy Creating Order

Weaknesses

  • May Seem Harsh
  • Workaholic Tendencies
  • Too Focused on Social Status

ESFJ - The Consul

Introduction

ESFJs are warm-hearted, social individuals who value harmony and enjoy helping others feel understood and appreciated.

Strengths

  • Community-Oriented
  • Strong Sense of Duty
  • Excellent Communicators

Weaknesses

  • Inflexible
  • Too Selfless
  • Often too Needy

EXPLORERS

ISTP - The Virtuoso

Introduction

ISTPs are action-oriented problem solvers who enjoy understanding how things work and solving practical issues.

Strengths

  • Observant
  • Calm Under Pressure
  • Handy and Resourceful

Weaknesses

  • Unapologetic
  • Gets Bored Easily
  • Commitment-Averse

ISFP - The Adventurer

Introduction

ISFPs are flexible and charming, always ready to explore and experience something new.

Strengths

  • Adventurous
  • Deeply Empathetic
  • Sensitive to Others

Weaknesses

  • Unpredictable
  • Can Easily be Stressed
  • Can be Difficult to Get to Know

ESTP - The Entrepreneur

Introduction

ESTPs are energetic and action-oriented individuals who thrive on excitement, challenges, and adventure.

Strengths

  • Fearless
  • Perceptive
  • Solution-Oriented

Weaknesses

  • Defiant
  • Impulsive
  • May Miss the Bigger Picture

ESFP - The Entertainer

Introduction

ESFPs are lively and spontaneous people who bring fun and excitement to any situation. They are naturally outgoing and enjoy the present moment.

Strengths

  • Sociable
  • Hands-on and Observant
  • Charismatic and Energetic

Weaknesses

  • Sensitive
  • Unfocused
  • Poor Long-Term Planners
